## 0.1.1 - May 7, 2021
### Added
- Added the following `Assert` methods:
  - `Assert.IsFalse(bool)`
  - `Assert.IsFalse(bool, string)`
  - `Assert.IsNull(obj)`
  - `Assert.IsNull(obj, string)`
  - `Assert.IsNotNull(obj)`
  - `Assert.IsNotNull(obj, string)`
